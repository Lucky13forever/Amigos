Frequently Asked Questions

If you have any other questions than the ones available here, do not hesitate to contact us via phone or social media.

  • Why should I switch to solar panels?

    Solar power systems derive clean, pure energy from the sun. Installing solar panels on your home lowers your costs over the time, helps combat greenhouse gas emissions and reduces our collective dependence on fossil fuel.

  • Can this panels be used for larger buildings?

    Of course, since the solar panels are modular, you can connect as many as you need. Don't hesitate to log in or sign up to find out how many panels you need.

  • What are the environmental benefits of solar energy?

    Solar power, like other renewable energy resources, has many environmental and health benefits. Going solar reduces greenhouse gas emissions, which contributes to climate change, and also results in fewer air pollutants such as sulfur dioxide and particulate matter, which can cause health problems.

  • How low are the costs of using a properly installed solar panel?

    During the summer, the costs are reduced by almost 90-100%, reaching the heating of the domestic water to be free. In spring and autumn these costs are reduced by at least 60%. Which makes a solar panel worth the investment.

  • How long will take to amortize the investment in solar panels?

    For a large household, a solar panel is not much more expensive than an electric or gas system. Depending on the location and the amount of domestic hot water used, the saving of electricity or natural gas differs, but it can be considered a payback period between 2-6 years.
